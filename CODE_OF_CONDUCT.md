# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, constructive, and evidence-focused community.

## Our Standard

We want this repository to be a place where people can:
- ask questions safely
- disagree about methods without hostility
- critique claims using evidence
- improve the benchmark and writeup without ego

## Expected Behavior

Please:
- be respectful and specific
- critique ideas, not people
- distinguish evidence from speculation
- avoid hype, personal attacks, and dismissive behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:
- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory language
- personal attacks
- bad-faith misrepresentation of results
- repeated disruption of discussion threads

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when needed to keep the project constructive and safe.
